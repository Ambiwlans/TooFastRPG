TooFastRPG


This is the English translation for 速すぎるRPG, a rather silly RPG made by KSB.

Popularized by youtube streamers like キヨ (Kiyo), 戌神ころね (Korone), ポッキー (Pocky), 兎田ぺこら (Pekora), and more.

The whole game is only around 30 minutes (too fast and too short). Have fun!
